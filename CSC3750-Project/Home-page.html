Lviv Handmade Chocolate Shop

Lviv Handmade Chocolate Shop offers variety of chocolate candies, chocolate figuires, bars, cakes, coffee. All sweets are made in this shop. Shop is located on second floor. You can also find the process of making chocolate.

Lviv Handmade Chocolate Cafe offers cakes and drinks that includes hot and cold, coffee and chocolate drinks. You can have table inside the cafe or outside on the terrace where you can enjoy the old Medieval town.

Lviv Handmade Chocolate Factory shows how all produce is made that is sold in this place. Chocolate Factory is on first and third floor.
